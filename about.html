About Me

Hello, I'm Vinicius Richter

I am a Forest Data Scientist with a background in Forest Engineering, working at the intersection of data science, machine learning, and statistics applied to the forestry sector. I hold a Master’s degree in Forest Engineering from UFSM, with a focus on forest growth and yield, geotechnologies, and the application of machine learning techniques for qualitative and quantitative forest monitoring. I am currently pursuing a Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science at IFFar, deepening my knowledge in programming and computational solution development.

Throughout my career, I have developed the Python library ForestPyTools and the QGIS plugin FPT PlotAllocation, aimed at optimizing planning and data processing in forest management projects. I have experience in geoprocessing, remote sensing, forest inventory, statistical modeling, and in developing machine learning and deep learning models for estimating forest attributes.

I have worked as a freelance data analyst, delivering solutions in data analysis, analytical workflow automation, BI dashboards, and computer vision applied to forest monitoring. I also gained industry experience in the forestry sector, with experience at companies such as CMPC Celulose Riograndense and Brasplan, working on forest inventory processing, neural network calibration, interactive dashboard development, and the integration of data science solutions into operational systems.

I am currently working as Forest Biometry Analyst – Level II (Forest Data Scientist) at Treevia Forest Technologies, where I am responsible for developing and maintaining statistical and data science–based solutions for forest data processing, including predictive modeling, LiDAR data processing, geoprocessing, analytical automation, and the continuous evaluation of new methodologies to support data-driven decision-making.

I am a curious professional, driven by continuous learning, with a strong focus on data science, machine learning, artificial intelligence, statistics, and the development of scalable solutions for real-world challenges in the forestry and environmental sectors.
