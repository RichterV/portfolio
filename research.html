Spectral images obtained by UAV with remote sensing and YOLOv8 in the inventory of Eucalyptus saligna Sm.

This study evaluates UAV imagery and the YOLOv8 model for detecting Eucalyptus saligna trees using vegetation indices. The method achieved high accuracy (F1-score of 0.98) six months after planting. The MPRI index was identified as the most efficient and cost-effective.
