Diametric modeling of five commercial species in aforest concession area in the Altamira National Forestin the Amazon biome

Analyzes the diameter distribution of five commercial tree species in the Altamira National Forest (Amazon), using forest census data and probability density functions. Highlights the presence of endangered species and supports sustainable forest management through statistical modeling.

UAV-based spectral images using remote sensing and YOLOv8 in Eucalyptus saligna Sm. inventory

This study evaluates UAV imagery and YOLOv8 for detecting Eucalyptus saligna trees using vegetation indices. The method achieved high accuracy (F1 score 0.98) six months after planting. MPRI was identified as the most efficient and cost-effective index.
